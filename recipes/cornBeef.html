Breakfast Sliders with Corned Beef and Eggs

Description:

Yet another way to use up leftover corned beef. The perfect grab-and-go breakfast for those hectic weekdays.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • Â¼ cup chopped corned beef
  • 2 tablespoons minced onion
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 slice Swiss cheese, cut into 4 pieces
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 slider buns, toasted

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degree F (190 degrees C)
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over low heat. Add corned beef and onions and cook, stirring occasionally, until onion is translucent, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Evenly portion out egg mixture over the bottom halves of the slider buns, add a piece of Swiss cheese, season with salt and pepper, and add the top halves of the buns. Serve warm.
